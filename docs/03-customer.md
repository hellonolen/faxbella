# FaxBella Customer Profile

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: Multi-Provider Medical Practice

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Industry** | Healthcare (medical, dental, veterinary) |
| **Size** | 3-50 providers |
| **Staff** | 5-100 employees |
| **Fax Volume** | 25-500 faxes/day |
| **Current Pain** | Manual fax sorting consuming 1-4+ hours/day |
| **Tech Sophistication** | Uses cloud EMR/EHR, email, basic SaaS |
| **Decision Maker** | Practice Manager or IT Lead |
| **Budget Authority** | $99-299/month approved without C-suite |
| **Location** | United States (HIPAA compliance) |

### Secondary ICP: Law Firms

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Size** | 5-50 attorneys |
| **Fax Volume** | 20-100 faxes/day |
| **Pain** | Document routing, matter attribution |
| **Decision Maker** | Office Manager or IT Director |

### Tertiary ICP: Professional Services

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Industries** | Insurance, Real Estate, Accounting |
| **Size** | 10-100 employees |
| **Pain** | Multi-department routing |

## Customer Personas

### Persona 1: Sarah, The Medical Office Manager

**Demographics**
- Age: 35-55
- Title: Office Manager, Practice Administrator
- Experience: 10+ years in medical admin
- Reports to: Practice Owner/Lead Physician

**Role & Responsibilities**
- Manages front desk staff (3-8 people)
- Oversees daily operations
- Ensures patient flow and satisfaction
- Handles vendor relationships
- Manages office technology

**Pain Points**
- "I spend the first 2 hours of every day sorting faxes"
- "Staff constantly interrupt me asking 'Where's that referral?'"
- "We've missed prior auth deadlines because faxes got buried"
- "I know there's a better way, but I don't have time to find it"

**Goals**
- Reduce fax-related chaos
- Free up staff for patient-facing work
- Avoid compliance issues
- Look competent to supervising physicians

**Tech Comfort**
- Uses EMR daily (Epic, Athena, eClinicalWorks)
- Comfortable with email and basic SaaS
- Will test new tools if setup is simple
- Needs clear ROI to propose purchases

**Decision Process**
- Researches solutions online (Google, peer recommendations)
- Requests demos, trials
- Presents to practice owner with cost/benefit
- Approved if savings > cost (easy math)

**Quote**
> "If you can prove it saves us 2 hours a day, I'll sign up today."

---

### Persona 2: Dr. Martinez, The Practice Owner

**Demographics**
- Age: 45-65
- Title: Physician Owner, Managing Partner
- Focus: Patient care, not operations

**Role & Responsibilities**
- Sees patients 4-5 days/week
- Reviews critical faxes (referrals, auths, results)
- Approves major purchases
- Sets practice direction

**Pain Points**
- "I keep asking why referrals aren't processed faster"
- "The staff seems busy but productivity is low"
- "I don't understand why faxes are still a problem in 2026"

**Goals**
- Practice runs smoothly without his intervention
- Staff handles operations efficiently
- Technology just works
- Avoid HIPAA violations

**Tech Comfort**
- Uses EMR, basic apps
- Delegates IT decisions to office manager
- Will approve any solution <$500/month that saves staff time

**Decision Process**
- Sarah presents solution
- He asks "What does it cost? What does it save?"
- If ROI is clear, approves immediately
- Delegates implementation

**Quote**
> "Just tell me it works and how much it costs. Sarah handles the rest."

---

### Persona 3: Mike, The IT Administrator

**Demographics**
- Age: 25-45
- Title: IT Manager, Systems Administrator
- Works at: Larger practice or healthcare group (20+ providers)

**Role & Responsibilities**
- Manages all technology
- Integrates systems
- Ensures security and compliance
- Evaluates new vendors

**Pain Points**
- "Our fax solution doesn't integrate with anything"
- "I need APIs and webhooks, not just email forwarding"
- "Security reviews take forever—I need HIPAA documentation upfront"

**Goals**
- Clean integrations with existing systems
- Reliable uptime and support
- Security that passes audits
- Automation that reduces tickets

**Tech Comfort**
- Very high
- Evaluates documentation, APIs, security posture
- Needs technical specs before recommending

**Decision Process**
- Evaluates multiple vendors
- Tests API, reviews SOC 2/HIPAA docs
- Recommends to practice leadership
- Implements and monitors

**Quote**
> "Give me good APIs, solid docs, and a BAA, and I'm sold."

## Anti-Personas (Who We Don't Serve)

### ❌ Solo Practitioners
- Receive <10 faxes/day
- No routing problem (they're the only recipient)
- Price sensitive

### ❌ Hospitals with In-House Fax Servers
- Already invested in Concord/RightFax
- Complex IT requirements
- 12-month procurement cycles

### ❌ Businesses Without Fax Problems
- Fully digital workflows
- No legacy fax senders
- Not our market
