## Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

### What is a BAA?

A Business Associate Agreement is required under HIPAA when a service provider handles Protected Health Information (PHI) on behalf of a covered entity.

### Our BAA Covers

| Obligation | Commitment |
|------------|-----------|
| PHI Protection | Implement safeguards per Security Rule |
| Use Limitations | Only use PHI for service delivery |
| Disclosure | No disclosure without authorization |
| Breach Notification | Report within 24 hours |
| Subcontractors | Require BAAs with all subcontractors |
| Access Rights | Support patient access requests |
| Termination | Return/destroy PHI on termination |

### How to Get a BAA

1. **Business Plan**: Standard BAA available on request
2. **Enterprise Plan**: BAA included; custom terms available
3. **Process**: Email legal@faxbella.com
