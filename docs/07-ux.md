# FaxBella User Experience

## UX Principles

1. **10-Minute Setup**: Any office manager can go from signup to first routed fax in 10 minutes
2. **Zero Training**: If it needs a tutorial, it's too complex
3. **Mobile-First Dashboard**: Works perfectly on phone/tablet for on-the-go
4. **Helpful Errors**: Every error explains what happened and how to fix it
5. **Delightful Details**: Micro-interactions that make the product feel premium

5. **Loading**: Skeleton screens, not spinners
6. **Success**: Confetti on first routed fax
